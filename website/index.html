About CiberMandi

A digital mandi built for transparency and trust

CiberMandi is a public digital mandi ecosystem that modernises agriculture trading. The platform digitises the entire mandi workflow – from gate entry and weighbridge to auction, payment and dispatch – so every step is transparent, traceable and efficient.

Farmers, traders and mandi authorities all see the same real-time information, which builds trust and ensures fair price discovery across all connected mandis.

How CiberMandi Works

From gate entry to final dispatch on one digital rail

CiberMandi connects every step of the mandi journey – farmer arrival, weighment, auction, settlement and dispatch – into a single, secure workflow. Each action creates a digital record, so boards, traders and farmers see the same truth.

1. Gate & registration

Vehicles, farmers and lots get digital gate slips, queue numbers and lot IDs as they enter the mandi.

2. Weighbridge

Gross and net weights are captured directly from electronic weighbridges, removing manual errors.

3. Digital auction

Traders bid digitally on each lot with full visibility of quality, base price and ranked bids.

4. Settlement & dispatch

Payouts, commissions, mandi fee and taxes are computed automatically, with digital receipts and dispatch slips.
